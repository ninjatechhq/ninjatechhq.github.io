Our Vision - BSCM, INC.

Our History

Founded in 1957, BSCM, INC. has been a leader in the scrap metal recycling industry of Callahan for over 65 years. We started as a small family operation and have grown into a trusted dealer in scrap metals, alloys, and catalytic converters.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality recycling services while promoting environmental sustainability and maximizing value for our customers through fair pricing and excellent service.

Our Values

Integrity, sustainability, customer satisfaction, and community responsibility drive everything we do at BSCM, INC. We provide fair pricing and honest dealing for all scrap metals, alloys, and catalytic converters.
