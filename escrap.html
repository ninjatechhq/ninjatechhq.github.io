Electronic Scrap Recycling Services

BSCM, INC. specializes in the responsible recycling of electronic waste. We handle everything from circuit boards to complete computer systems, ensuring proper disposal and maximum value recovery.

Circuit Boards & Components

We purchase high-grade circuit boards, memory modules, processors, and electronic components. Our team evaluates each item to ensure you receive the best possible price for your electronic scrap.

  • High-grade motherboards
  • Memory RAM modules
  • CPU processors
  • Hard drives and SSDs

Computer & Server Equipment

Complete computer systems, servers, and networking equipment accepted. We handle bulk quantities from business upgrades and data center decommissioning projects.

  • Desktop computers
  • Laptop computers
  • Server equipment
  • Networking hardware

Mobile Devices & Tablets

Smartphones, tablets, and mobile devices contain valuable materials. We provide secure data destruction and environmentally responsible recycling of all mobile electronics.

  • Smartphones (all brands)
  • Tablets and iPads
  • E-readers
  • Smartwatches

Electronic Components

Individual electronic components and specialized equipment. We evaluate precious metal content and provide competitive pricing for electronic waste of all types.

  • Connectors & cables
  • Power supplies
  • Electronic transformers
  • Industrial electronics

Secure Data Destruction

Professional data destruction services ensure your sensitive information is completely eliminated. We provide certificates of destruction for compliance and peace of mind.

  • Hard drive destruction
  • SSD secure wiping
  • Certificate of destruction
  • HIPAA compliance

Bulk Commercial Services
